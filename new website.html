Swami Vivekananda
Swami Vivekananda, born Narendranath Datta was an Indian Hindu monk,
philosopher, author, religious teacher,
and the chief disciple of the Indian mystic Ramakrishna.
He was a key figure in the introduction of Vedanta and Yoga to the Western world.
Mahatma Gandhi
Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi was an Indian lawyer, anti-colonial nationalist,
and political ethicist who employed nonviolent resistance to lead the successful campaign
for India's independence from British rule.
He inspired movements for civil rights and freedom across the world
Subhas Chandra Bose
Subhas Chandra Bose was an Indian nationalist
whose defiance of British authority in India made him
a hero among many Indians,
but his wartime alliances with Nazi Germany
and Fascist Japan left a legacy vexed by authoritarianism,
anti-Semitism, and military failure.

was one of the leading figures of the Indian Rebellion of 1857.

she assumed leadership of Jhansi after the outbreak of conflict
and fought several battles against the British.
